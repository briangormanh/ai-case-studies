Our client
Why this matters
ChatGPT searched Bing, found her content ranking well, and included her in the AI-generated answer. This is exactly how AI search works—it pulls from the same ranked results we optimize for.

We didn't optimize for ChatGPT. We optimized for search engines. ChatGPT found her because she ranks.
The approach

30-Minute Expert Interviews. 3 Months of Lead Goals Hit.

What we did
  • Conducted 30-minute interviews with Dr. Orr about what she actually tells patients
  • Turned conversations into structured, expert-sourced content
  • Focused on real questions patients ask—not keyword-stuffed topics
  • Applied standard on-page SEO: headings, internal links, schema
What we didn't do
  • No AI-specific optimization or "GEO" tactics
  • No schema markup targeting AI platforms
  • No prompt engineering or LLM submission
  • No special content formatting for AI extraction
The point
These two SME pages ramped from zero to generating the majority of all site traffic within weeks. After they launched, Dr. Orr hit her monthly lead goal three consecutive months—proving this wasn't just visibility, it was qualified traffic. And the AI visibility? A byproduct of strong fundamentals, not a separate effort.
